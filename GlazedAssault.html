Summary

GlazedAssault is a 2D action-packed shoot 'em up where the player must survive a relentless assault of croissant enemies in a single-screen arena. Players manage a combo meter to unleash powerful abilities and outlast increasingly difficult waves.

Core Systems

  • Character Movement & Controls – Implement smooth and responsive player movement for dodging enemies and navigating the environment.
  • Player Input Handling – Manage keyboard and mouse controls for character movement, aiming, and firing projectiles.
  • Wave Management – Dynamically spawn and manage enemy types and numbers, with difficulty escalating across successive waves.

Gameplay Breakdown

Objectives & Missions

  • The primary objective is survival. The player must defeat all enemies in a wave to advance to the next, with the ultimate goal of surviving all five waves of enemies.

Combat Mechanics

  • Engage in fast-paced projectile-based combat against a variety of enemies.
  • Manage character health and strategically use firepower to clear each wave.

Combo & Power-up System

  • Build a combo multiplier by continuously destroying enemies.
  • High combo counts unlock powerful, screen-clearing abilities providing a temporary advantage.

Progression & Feedback Loop

  • Survive each wave to face increasingly difficult enemies.
  • The combo meter provides visual feedback for combat effectiveness.

Endgame or Loop Reset

  • The game concludes either by completing all waves or if the player's health reaches zero.
  • Game-over allows players to restart and attempt to beat their best time.
