    • Summary

      Frogdoku is a 3D grid-based puzzle game where logic, planning, and spatial awareness are key. As Konna, a clever frog, your mission is to arrange lily pads into a viable path, then leap across them to safety.

      Merge logic with rhythm as you arrange lily pads into harmonious patterns that unlock your path. With stylized visuals, ambient sound, and fluid movement, the experience is both relaxing and mentally engaging.

      Puzzles start simple but quickly evolve into intricate brain teasers where every move can lead to victory—or a splash! Inspired by the elegance of Sudoku and the movement logic of block puzzles, Frogdoku offers a rewarding challenge for puzzle enthusiasts.
