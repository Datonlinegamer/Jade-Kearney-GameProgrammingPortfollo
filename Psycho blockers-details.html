Summary

Psycho Blockers is a 2.3D hack-and-slash, side-scrolling game that blends fast-paced action with strategic combat. Players must navigate, battle, and strategize to overcome waves of enemies and complex environmental challenges.

Code Breakdown

This section covers wall running and stuck recovery logic for the player character:

  • Wall Running: Activated when the player is near a wall using raycasts.
  • UpdateWallRun() checks all ground raycast points each frame.

If wallRun is active and the player is airborne, gravity is reduced and vertical velocity slows.

Gameplay Breakdown

Psycho Blockers combines fluid combat, traversal mechanics, and resource management systems into a cohesive gameplay loop.

Objectives & Missions

  • Collect resources such as plants and metal scraps for crafting.

Combat Mechanics

  • Engage enemies using directional slashes and combo-based attacks.

Resource Management & Crafting

  • Gather materials from the environment or defeated enemies.
  • Craft weapons, tools, and consumables to enhance survival.

Progression & Feedback Loop

  • Gather resources → Craft items → Upgrade abilities/weapons.
  • Defeat enemies and bosses → Unlock new areas and skills.

Endgame or Loop Reset

  • Completing objectives unlocks new challenges or levels.
  • Respawn or restart mechanics allow players to refine performance.
