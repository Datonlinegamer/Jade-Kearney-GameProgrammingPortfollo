    Summary

    MindScape is a procedurally generated game where you control a drone, navigating past enemies and locked doors while leveling up, crafting items, and ultimately seeking to defeat the Bwitch.

    Core Systems

    • Character Movement & Controls – Implement smooth and responsive player movement.
    • Camera Systems – Ensure proper camera tracking and transitions.
    • Player Input Handling – Manage controls for movement, combat, and interactions.
    • Physics & Animation Integration – Integrate physics-based interactions and animations for fluid gameplay.

    Gameplay Breakdown

    Objectives & Missions

    • The player follows quests and missions (Find and kill Time Bwitch).
    • Collects resources such as plants and metal scraps for crafting.

    Exploration & Interaction

    • Navigate the environment, overcoming obstacles and locked doors.
    • Interact with crafting stations, doors, and enemies.

    Combat Mechanics

    • Engage in combat using weapons or abilities.
    • Manage health through healing items or recovery systems.

    Resource Management & Crafting

    • Collect materials from the environment or defeated enemies.
    • Craft weapons, tools, and consumables to enhance survival.

    Progression & Feedback Loop

    • Gather resources → Craft items → Upgrade abilities/weapons.
    • Defeat enemies and bosses → Unlock new areas and abilities.

    Endgame or Loop Reset

    • Completing objectives unlocks new challenges or levels.
    • A respawn or game-over mechanic triggers if the player dies.
