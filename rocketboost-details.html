Summary

Rocket Boost is a physics driven game where the player pilot a retro futuristic rocket through obstacle filled environments using precise booster control and momentum. The objective is to collect fuel consumables and special items scattered across each level while avoiding hazards, managing limited fuel, and mastering aerial navigation.
